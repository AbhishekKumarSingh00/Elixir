About Us

Elixir is the first-ever Health Society of a college in India! The Elixir is an initiative started by students who care not only about their health but of those around them as well. Health is something that often gets neglected in our competitive student life. Through Elixir we wish to promote the wellbeing of both, the physical and mental aspects of health and build an energetic, productive lifestyle. In 2019 it is expanded to health & fitness society of DTU
